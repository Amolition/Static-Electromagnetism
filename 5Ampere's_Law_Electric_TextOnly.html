$$ \nabla \times \mathbf{E} = 0$$

The curl of $\mathbf{E}$ is 0 because it a conservative field. A conservative field can be described by a potential such that $\mathbf{E} = \nabla \phi$. The curl of the gradient of a scalar field ($\nabla \times \nabla \phi$) is 0; Hence, the curl of $\mathbf{E}$ is 0.
